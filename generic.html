More About Me!

I am a current Junior at the University of Illinois at Chicago. I am majoring in Computer Science and Minoring in Business Administration. I am enrolling in UIC's joint Bachelors and Masters Program and plan to graduate with both in May 2027.

I am a first generation Palestinian American college student. Some of my hobbies include Palestinian Embroidery, Running, Room Design, and Fashion.

Some of my goals include finding a career in Project Management or Software Engineering, as well as finding a company that aligns with my personal values and skills

I have partaken in a variety of projects and extracurriculars. Some including being active in the Palestinian Children Reliefs Fund, ACM Mobile Development Club, as well as Women in Computer Science Programs.

Some of my Achievements include consectively making the Dean's List, Being a Scholarship Recipient of the Chanceller Laurettes, Presidents Award Program, and Chicago Engineers Foundation. As well as being an Illinois State Scholar.
